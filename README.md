## 🌟 What is StreamWhere?

Imagine you want to watch a super cool new movie, but you don't know if it's on Netflix, Prime Video, Disney+, or somewhere else. It can be super annoying to open every single app just to check, right? 

That's where **StreamWhere** comes to the rescue! 🦸‍♂️ 

All you have to do is type the name of the movie, and we will tell you **exactly** which app has it! It's like having a magic wand for your movie nights! ✨

<br />

## ✨ Awesome Features

*   **🔍 Super Fast Search:** Just type a few letters, and boom! The movie appears before you even finish typing!
*   **😲 Smart Magic Glow:** The search bar literally glows with atmospheric light depending on how you interact with it. 
*   **📱 Works on Everything:** You can use StreamWhere easily on your phone, your reading tablet, or your computer screen.
*   **🔥 Trending Now:** See what movies all your friends and people around the world are watching right now!
*   **🔒 Safe and Private:** We keep things safe and simple so you can focus on finding the best movies easily.

<br />

## 🚀 How to Use It

1.  **Open the App**: Just visit our website.
2.  **Search**: Click on the big shiny search bar at the very top.
3.  **Type**: Type a movie name (like "Batman", "Spider-Man", or "Inception").
4.  **Click**: Click on the movie poster that pops up.
5.  **Watch**: Look for the logos (like Netflix or Prime Video) to see exactly where to watch it!

It's as simple as 1, 2, 3! 🎈
