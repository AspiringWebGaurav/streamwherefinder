## 🎯 Getting Started

StreamWhere is designed to be frictionless. You do **not** need to create an account to start searching for movies. It works right out of the box!

### 1. The Global Search Feature
The core of StreamWhere is the **Intelligent Glow Search Bar**.
*   **Where is it?** It's located prominently at the top of the homepage, and nested in the global navigation bar on every other page.
*   **How does it work?** Just type the name of a movie (e.g., "The Matrix" or "Inception"). As you type, the engine instantly ranks and pulls the most relevant global TMDB matches.
*   **Atmospheric Glow:** You'll notice the search bar reacts to you. This glow acts as a soft ambient guide and shifts depending on how frequently you use the search.

### 2. Viewing Where to Watch
Once you click on a movie from the search results, you are taken to the **Movie Detail Page**.
*   **The Breakdown:** Here, you will see a detailed breakdown of where you can **Stream**, **Buy**, or **Rent** the movie.
*   **Providers:** We pull live data for providers like Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, Apple TV+, Hulu, and more!
*   **Click & Go:** Click on a provider logo to be immediately securely directed to their app/website to watch the movie.

### 3. Exploring Collections
Don't know what to watch? We've got you covered.
*   **Trending in India:** See what's currently popular regionally.
*   **Popular Worldwide:** Check out the globally acclaimed blockbusters.
*   **Upcoming Capabilities:** We categorize movies into neat horizontal scrolling carousels on the homepage for easy browsing.

### 4. Optional: Creating an Account (Firebase Secure)
If you want to save your progress or sync your search history across devices:
1. Click the **Profile Icon / Login Button** in the top right corner.
2. Sign in securely using **Google Authentication** (No passwords to remember or lose!).
3. Your search history is now safely synced to your account.

## ⚙️ Pro-Tips & Settings
*   **Language Support:** Click the global globe icon in the navigation bar to swap between built-in localized languages.
*   **Clear History:** If you are logged in, you can wipe your entire search history instantly from your Profile Settings page.
